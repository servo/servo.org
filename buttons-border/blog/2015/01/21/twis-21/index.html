This Week In Servo 21

What's up with Servo for the week of 21 - 27 Jan 2015

Posted 2015-01-21

This week, we merged 8 pull requests. Not many significant changes this week since people were busy with the rustup and we try not to land large changes that need to be rebased over.

Ms2ger reached his 1000th commit, and is the first on the project to do so!

Notable additions

  • The rust upgrade is still going on; it has now reached review.
  • Simon landed the new CSS parser

Meeting

Minutes

We discussed setting up CI for our dependencies, whether or not they should all track Rust nightly, and the rust upgrade.
