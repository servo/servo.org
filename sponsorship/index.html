Sponsorship

You can support the development of Servo project in different ways.

By donating to the LFX crowdfunding page for Servo, you can support the project’s infrastructure and hosting costs. The money there is used to keep the project up and running, including CI bots and related hardware requirements for the project.

If your organization is interested in sponsoring Servo's development or the implementation of a particular feature, reach out to us at join@servo.org.


Servo Project Fund

The purpose of the Servo Project Fund is to raise, budget, and spend funds in support of the Servo Project.

The Fund is governed by the Directed Fund’s Governing Board at the Linux Foundation. The Board is composed of Members, together with representation from the Linux Foundation and the Technical Steering Committee.

There are currently no members of the Linux Foundation Directed Fund’s Governing Board.

If your organization is interested in becoming a member, please contact us at join@servo.org or enroll at the Linux Foundation member enrollment page.
