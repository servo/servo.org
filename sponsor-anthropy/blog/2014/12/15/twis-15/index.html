What's up with Servo for the week of 10 Dec - 16 Dec 2014

Posted 2014-12-15

This week, we merged 41 pull requests

Notable additions

  • Patrick implemented word-spacing, overflow-wrap/word-wrap, outline, letter-spacing, box-shadow, text-indent, and legacy bgcolor/border.
  • Michael landed a gonk port
  • Chris improved our rooting safety plugins to ignore unsafe blocks
  • Matt Rasmus implemented form resetting
  • Ms2ger cleaned up page.rs and some manual deref()/deref_mut() calls, as well as removing a bad transmute()
  • Michael added support for rem
  • Patrick fixed a double borrow and some race conditions
