## Rust Nation 23

Servo is a **web rendering engine** written in **Rust**.

<div style="font-size: 0.6em;">

* Started by Mozilla in 2012.  
* Moved to the Linux Foundation in 2020.  
* In 2023 a team of developers are actively working on Servo.

</div>
